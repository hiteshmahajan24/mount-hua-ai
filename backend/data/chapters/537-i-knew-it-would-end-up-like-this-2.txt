The high priest’s body staggered and fell back.

Blood constantly flowed down his chest, as if the qi flowed in reverse and leaked from there.

No one remained standing.

The exhausted Mount Hua disciples had all fallen on the ground, and the high priest couldn’t stand anymore either.

Even Chung Myung, on his knees, could barely remain standing with his sword as support.

“Heuk…. Heuk… heuk…”

Chung Myung’s mouth heaved with heavy breaths.

Pressing down on the sword, he struggled to get up, then limped to the fallen high priest.

More blood flowed from the open chest wound each time the high priest coughed or tried to adjust his throat. It seemed that, no matter what anyone could say, even if the heavenly beings themselves intervened, he couldn’t be saved.

Even Chung Myung, who had exhausted all his strength, was no exception. He stumbled as he used his sword as a makeshift walking stick to approach the high priest, collapsing on the spot as if his strength had vanished. His broken leg prevented him from going any further.

However, gritting his teeth, he pushed himself up with trembling arms, his eyes burning with anger. Chung Myung dragged his injured leg as he neared the high priest.

To steal the breath of the high priest.

But then, at that very moment.

The high priest’s hand clenched the earth, and his body leaped up in a deceitful motion.

Chung Myung was taken aback.

The high priest’s eyes, now standing, lacked focus. It appeared as if his mind had departed, and he ascended with vacant, dim eyes.

It was merely the final phase, like a candle that blazes before extinguishing, the ultimate exhalation of life for those who have exhausted their existence.

As soon as Chung Myung grasped the sword and attempted to rise, the high priest pivoted and stumbled toward the cave.

While Chung Myung endeavored to approach, the members of the Demonic Sect, who had managed to barely breach the Ice Palace warriors’ barrier, obstructed his path and valiantly protected the high priest.

“UHHHH! High priest! Ackkkk!”

Their desperate cries pierced the ears, causing terrible agony. Chung Myung could feel their madness and willingness to protect the high priest, even if it meant sacrificing their sight.

Chung Myung clenched his teeth and trembled. Wasn’t it starting to seem like he was the villain now?

With a shaky voice, the high priest addressed the members of the Demonic Sect.

“I… I will go back…”

“We’ll hold them off! Go!”

The high priest stumbled towards the cave.

Chung Myung’s face contorted in terror.

His intention wasn’t solely to defeat the high priest but rather to thwart the resurrection of the Heavenly Demon.

Even if they managed to eliminate the high priest, all of their efforts would be futile if the Heavenly Demon came back to life.

However, he no longer possessed the power to overpower the demons. All he could do was remain conscious.

Chung Myung smirked.

“I am also a fool. To consider obstacles to be insurmountable.”

There are things that need to happen in the world, and of course, it wasn’t a choice.

At the moment when Chung Myung was about to raise his sword.

With a desperate shout, someone leaped in front of him and commenced attacking the Demonic Sect members.

Chung Myung looked blankly at the scene. It was a familiar face. It was Song Won, the leader of the Ice Palace guards, who once guided Mount Hua disciples to the mine. With his body already covered in blood and cuts, he rushed to the demons.

His desperate shout filled the valley.

“You stupid bastards! How long are you planning to leave the fate of the Ice Palace to the outsiders! If there is something we need to protect, we do it with our own hands!”

And it wasn’t only Song Won.

Seol So-Baek, who came running while holding his sword, charged towards the demons.

At the sight, Chung Myung could only curse.

This member of the Demonic Sect rushed towards Seol So-Baek. However, fortunately, Han Yi-Myung ran ahead and blocked him just in time.

“What are you doing!”

His shout was directed at their warriors.

“Risk your life to open the path up! Open the damned path for Taoist Chung Myung and stain it with the blood of the Ice Palace! If there’s any loyalty and spirit left in you, prove it now!”

The eyes of the Ice Palace burned with anger. This was their battle.

But these Central Plains people, who had no connection to them, shed their blood and risked their lives for their cause.

If they had no shame after witnessing this, they could no longer be considered human. And if their blood didn’t boil upon seeing such a fight, they could not be warriors.

They wouldn’t feel this much shame if there was even a single reprimand or a single rebuke. But the disciples of Mount Hua were fighting without accusing the Ice Palace of anything.

This fact prevented the Ice Palace warriors from staying passive.

“Unleash the power of Taoist Chung Myung!”

“Risk your life without hesitation!”

The warriors of Ice Palace launched a fierce assault against the Demonic Sect members, displaying a newfound strength.

No longer paralyzed by fear and doubt, they were determined to confront their adversaries head-on.

A wave of killing intent emanated from them as they fearlessly stared down their opponents and unleashed their attacks. They were willing to endure any wounds if it meant clearing the path quickly.

Seol So-Baek and Han Yi-Myung supported Chung Myung as he stumbled.

Chung Myung glanced at them, grateful for their assistance, and nodded. Without speaking, he sprinted forward.

“Die! You spawns of the Demonic Sect!”

“This is the North Sea! Not a place for the likes of you!”

Despite being impaled by the hands of the Demonic Sect, they refused to retreat, wielding their swords.

A transformation in each of them.

Nothing could be accomplished by deferring the responsibility to others and taking care of oneself in solitude. A greater sacrifice was being made.

Those who acknowledged it started forging a path, even at the cost of their lives. The fiercely resisting Demonic Sect was gradually being pushed aside. The road to the cave opened faster than Chung Myung could even run.

Some who had taken the lead ahead of Chung Myung acted as if they were his escorts.

Chung Myung didn’t even check behind him. All that remained was to entrust this matter to the Ice Palace and do what he needed to do.

The elites of the Ice Palace and Chung Myung entered the cave, witnessing the collapse all around them. However, a path was still available inside. They were unsure if this was fortunate or not.

“He can’t go too far in that condition!”

Surely, he must be limping around without utilizing footwork. Therefore, they could easily catch up even if the distance increased slightly. However, they soon realized that the process of catching up would not be as simple as they initially thought.

“There is something up ahead!”

The members of the Demonic Sect should no longer be in the cave. However, there were indications of movement within, and it wasn’t just a single entity.

Those with keen senses involuntarily gritted their teeth at the chilling sensation emanating from ahead.

Han Yi-Myung widened his eyes and gazed at the approaching figures.

Song Won pressed his lips together.

The individuals with hazy eyes started to stumble towards them.

“Don’t retreat! Strike them!”

Someone bellowed, their face contorted in an otherworldly manner.

“Not a human being! That… that’s not a human being! Cursed place!”

The attire of these figures undoubtedly belonged to the North Sea.

‘The missing people…’

It was clear that these people had kidnapped the citizens of the North Sea. And because they were walking on their feet, they tried to sense signs of life but found nothing.

How could they do such a disgusting thing!

“I-is that a gangshi?”

Chung Myung mumbled softly.

Everyone was certain that this was a terrible thing, but Chung Myung noticed something peculiar about them.

The dead ones transformed into gangshi lack limb manipulation abilities. It was evident that these people were captured for a mysterious experiment.

However, a gangshi was merely a gangshi. It was not an exaggeration to say that this one, crafted from the iciness of the North Sea, was not a cursed monster like the others.

Song Won shouted passionately.

“Don’t waste any time! I’ll take care of this! Go on!”

Han Yi-Myung nodded in agreement.

Simultaneously, the guards of the Ice Palace sprung forward.

The sound of swords colliding with lifeless bodies resounded. And then, the steel hands of the gangshi tore through the guard’s skin.

Desperately, the guard screamed and started to retreat. The gangshi, without any hint of reason, were wholly focused on pursuing the Ice Palace warriors ahead, paying no attention to the unfolding chaos.

Chung Myung dashed forward, his expression chilling.

‘This is a complete mess!’

The boiling hatred and anger seemed to engulf his heart, as the battle with the Demonic Sect had always been this way.

“There is a way ahead!”

Han Yi-Myung’s eyes were red as the Ice Palace warriors cried out.

“Go! Taoist Chung Myung! Go!”

Even this time, they were outnumbered. However, Han Yi-Myung said it with determination.

“We will risk our lives to break through! Move forward!”

“Move forward! Taoist!”

At Chung Myung’s hesitation, Seol So-Baek shouted, looking determined, which made Chung Myung nod.

“He has now become a soldier.”

War turned children into adults.

In a way, this was an unfortunate thing, but in this place, he had to acknowledge the kid as a soldier.

All the other soldiers of Ice Palace rushed to block the dead and made a path forward. It was a path for just one person to enter, and Chung Myung squeezed through it as they stumbled ahead.

Blood trickled down his hand, splattering onto the floor. His vision blurred, and he could no longer feel his fingers clenching.

Despite the absence of pain, he forced his body to move. He had to keep walking.

Chung Myung’s head jerked up, drawing his attention to the menacing growl of some savage creature. More dead were approaching.

That man really was meticulous.

Chung Myung’s smile twisted into a sinister grin.

“I apologize… because I never give up.”

Chung Myung quietly pressed forward, hoisting the sword that now felt heavier than a thousand pounds. Just then, he heard a voice he recognized all too well.

Chung Myung glanced back.

“Those who can’t find a place to meet their end are doomed to live in chaos. But now, I’ve found my place to die.”

Chung Myung gazed at Yo Sa-Heon with empty eyes. The old man’s face appeared tranquil.

“What more can I say? Go.”

Perhaps it would be wiser for Chung Myun to move ahead alone, so he did, but Yo Sa-Heon didn’t consider it.

His mission was to prevent the resurrection of Heavenly Demon and…

‘Retrieve the lost spirit of the North Sea.’

And in order to accomplish that, someone like Chung Myung should be the one alive, not him.

The qi surged from him as he forcefully shoved the gangshi. However, this time, the number of opponents had increased, and more gangshi were pressuring Chung Myung.

“Keep moving! Don’t glance back!”

Yo Sa-Heon diligently blocked them all, and upon hearing the shout from behind, Chung Myung refused to look back.

The cave, which had initially appeared short, now stretched so far that he couldn’t discern its end.

With every step, his leg refused to halt, weakening his body and blurring his vision. All he could hear was his own breath.

Ultimately, Chung Myung dropped to his knees and grazed the ground with his bare hands as though scratching it.

There had been numerous occasions in the past where things were tougher than this. It had been more agonizing than this. Teeth clenched, Chung Myung hoisted his sagging body.

‘I am the Plum Blossom Sword Saint of Mount Hua.’

He dragged his motionless legs, slowly advancing along the narrow, dark path.

How long had he been moving like this?

A faint light could be seen in that never-ending path that seemed to have no final destination. He could make out the colossal figure of an Asura and the high priest kneeling before him, their bodies inhuman.

Chung Myung stumbled toward the high priest, dragging his sword.

“Second… Coming… of the Heavenly Demon…”

He chanted with great difficulty.

“Receive this body of mine and descend… into the world, eventually set the world on fire….”

There was no time to stop; the high priest’s wrist was being soaked into the floor, right on the ritual circle. And soon after, it started to emit a red glow.

Return of the Mount Hua Sect
